About Event

Career camp 1.0 is being organized by the IEEE society of IIT Mandi. Through this camp, we aim to let the new generation of engineers become aware of the opportunities that IEEE provides. The IEEE Day is held every year on the 5th of October and to celebrate this event we have invited renowned speakers from all over India to teach the new generation of innovators. The camp will run for two days 9th - 10th October each day featuring two keynote speakers. To be a part of this camp register yourself using the link below. Certificates would be provided to the attendees
