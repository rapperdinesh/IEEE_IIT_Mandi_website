Event 2:

As a part of Women’s day celebration WiE Affinity Group of IEEE SB Chapter IIT Mandi organised a Talent Showcase competition during the second week of March, 2021 (8-11 March, 2021). It was an event held so as to recognise and appreciate the hidden talents of females in our institute and was open to all females of the institute. It expected each participant to submit a 2-minute recorded video in which they would be performing their talent. The event saw a huge number of participants and made it really competitive for the judge to decide the winners. The entries included various talented videos showing the cultural, calligraphy, photography and many other skills of the talented females. Top 3 were selected and awarded along with 2 special mentions.



Event 3:

As a part of Women’s day celebration WiE Affinity Group of IEEE SB Chapter IIT Mandi in collaboration with Designauts Club, IIT Mandi organised a Poster designing competition during the second week of March, 2021 (8-13 March, 2021). It was an event held so as to recognise and appreciate the poster designing skills of students in our institute and was open to all students of the institute. It expected all the participants to submit a softcopy of their final posters that they designed on the topic ‘Indian Women in STEM’. The event saw a huge number of participants and made it really competitive for the judge to decide the winners. The results were decided by the Designauts Club and included Winner up, Runner up and Special mention.
