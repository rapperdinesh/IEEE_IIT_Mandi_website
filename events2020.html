HARDWARE HACKATHON:  HACKTRONICS

Hacktronics is a Hardware Hackathon being organised by Aavesh Electronics Association of IIIT Una,in collaboration with the IEEE Student Branch, IIT Mandi.
The 48 hour hackathon is to be conducted on 11th and 12th April,where students can participate in teams to design and build some innovative hardware project over a period of two days.
Hacktronics 2020 has been thought to be an event with no restrictions on themes and projects under all categories can be registered.
The interested students can register in teams on or before March 15th, 2020 using the link provided below: https://bit.ly/2OHfRUa

Workshop

The IEEE PELS SB Chapter, IIT Mandi is organizing a two days' workshop on Raspberry Pi on March 7-8th, 2020 (Venue will be notified soon). The scheduled workshop shall be delivered by an industry expert, that would include a short session on Linux OS and Python followed by significant hands-on sessions. The workshop will cover basic to advance level concept on the Raspberry Pi development board though the application in the Internet of Things (IoT) and Home automation.
The workshop is open to all. A certificate will be provided after successful completion of the course.
